NHS Agenda for Change

The Agenda for Change (AfC) pay system covers most NHS staff in England. It has 9 pay bands, each with multiple pay points that staff progress through annually. Band 1 is being phased out, with most entry-level roles now starting at Band 2. Higher Cost Area Supplements apply in London and surrounding areas.

Civil Service Pay

Civil service pay varies by department, as each department sets its own pay ranges within a common framework. The grades shown are approximate ranges ‚Äî actual pay depends on your department, role, and location. Common grades run from Administrative Assistant (AA) to Senior Civil Servant (SCS).

Local Government NJC

The National Joint Council (NJC) for Local Government Services sets pay scales for council staff in England, Wales and Northern Ireland. The "Green Book" scale uses Spinal Column Points (SCPs) grouped into grades by individual councils.

About These Figures

Pay figures shown are approximate and based on publicly available 2024/25 scales with estimated 2025/26 uplifts. Always check with your employer or union for confirmed rates.
