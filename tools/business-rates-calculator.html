How Business Rates Are Calculated

Your business rates bill = Rateable Value √ó Multiplier ‚àí Reliefs. The rateable value is set by the Valuation Office Agency based on estimated annual rental value. The multiplier (pence in the pound) is set by central government annually.

2025/26 Multipliers

The standard multiplier for 2025/26 is 55.5p (for properties with RV above ¬£51,000). The small business multiplier is 49.9p (for properties with RV at or below ¬£51,000). These are applied per pound of rateable value.

Small Business Rate Relief

Properties with a rateable value of ¬£12,000 or less get 100% relief (no business rates to pay). Between ¬£12,001 and ¬£15,000, relief tapers from 100% to 0% on a sliding scale. You must occupy only one property to qualify.

Empty Property Relief

Empty commercial properties are exempt for the first 3 months (6 months for industrial properties). After that, full rates apply. Some properties may qualify for ongoing empty property relief.
