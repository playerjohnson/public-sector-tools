How Bin Collections Work

Most UK councils collect bins on a fortnightly rota, alternating between general waste and recycling. Food waste is typically collected weekly. Garden waste collections may be seasonal (March‚ÄìNovember) and some councils charge for this service (typically ¬£35‚Äì¬£50 per year).

Bank Holiday Changes

Bin collections often shift by one day after bank holidays. If your collection falls on a Monday bank holiday, it will usually be collected on Tuesday, with each subsequent day pushed forward. Most councils publish revised schedules on their website around bank holiday periods.

Missed Collections

If your bin was out on time but wasn't collected, report it to your council. Most allow you to report missed collections online, and they'll arrange a return visit within a few days. Don't put extra waste next to the bin ‚Äî crews may not take it.
